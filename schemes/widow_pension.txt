Scheme Name: Indira Gandhi National Widow Pension Scheme (IGNWPS)

Details:
IGNWPS is a non-contributory pension scheme implemented by the Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India. It provides financial support to widows from poor families (Below Poverty Line - BPL) as a part of social security.

Benefits:
- ₹300 per month for widows aged 40–79 years
- ₹500 per month for widows aged 80 years and above

Eligibility:
- The applicant must be a widow aged between 40 and 79 years.
- The applicant must belong to a Below Poverty Line (BPL) household as per Government of India criteria.

Exclusions:
Pension will be discontinued if:
- The widow remarries
- The household is no longer Below Poverty Line (BPL)

Application Process:
Online / Offline

Online:
1. Download the UMANG App or visit [https://web.umang.gov.in/web_new/home](https://web.umang.gov.in/web_new/home)
2. Login using mobile number and OTP
3. Search for “NSAP”
4. Click “Apply Online”
5. Fill in details, select pension payment method, upload photo
6. Submit the application

Documents Required:
- Husband’s Death Certificate (with the name of the surviving wife)
- BPL Card
- Age Proof:
  • Birth certificate / School certificate preferred  
  • If unavailable, Ration Card / Voter ID (EPIC) can be used  
  • In absence of any, a government medical officer can issue an age certificate
