Scheme Name: Ayushman Bharat – Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY)

Launched: 23rd September 2018  
By: Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi  
Part of: National Health Policy 2017


OVERVIEW:

Ayushman Bharat is a flagship initiative under the National Health Policy 2017 to achieve Universal Health Coverage (UHC). It is aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and ensures "no one is left behind".

It comprises two components:
1. Health and Wellness Centres (HWCs)
2. Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY)

PM-JAY is the largest health assurance scheme in the world, offering ₹5,00,000 annual health cover per family for secondary and tertiary care, covering 10.74 crore poor families (~50 crore individuals).

 
BENEFITS:

- ₹5,00,000 cashless cover per family/year
- No cap on family size, age, or gender
- Pre-existing diseases covered from day one
- Family floater basis: any family member can use
- Covers:
  - Pre- and post-hospitalization (15 days follow-up)
  - Medical consultation, surgery, diagnostics, ICU
  - Medicines, implants, food, accommodation


ELIGIBILITY:


Rural Families:
- Only one room with kucha roof/walls
- No adult between 16–59 years
- Female-headed household with no male adult
- Disabled member with no able-bodied adult
- SC/ST households
- Landless households depending on manual labour
- Automatically included: destitute, manual scavengers, bonded labourers, primitive tribal groups

Urban Workers (any one of the following):
- Ragpicker, beggar
- Domestic worker, cobbler, hawker, vendor
- Construction worker, plumber, mason, labourer
- Sanitation worker, sweeper, gardener
- Handicraft worker, tailor, artisan
- Driver, cart puller, rickshaw puller
- Waiter, delivery assistant, helper
- Mechanic, electrician, repair worker
- Washer-man, chowkidar

Exclusions:
- Owns a 2/3/4-wheeler or motorized boat
- Mechanized farming equipment
- Kisan credit card limit over ₹50,000
- Government employee / PSU / salaried over ₹10,000/month
- Owns fridge, landline, decent pucca house, or 5+ acres land


APPLICATION PROCESS (Offline)

Visit hospital or CSC (Common Service Centre). Arogya Mitra assists the process.

Step 1: Submit PM letter / RSBY URN / ration card / mobile no.  
Step 2: BIS database search by operator  
Step 3: Upload ID documents – Aadhaar, ration card, etc.  
Step 4: Family verification and document upload  
Step 5: Health agency reviews and approves/rejects  
Step 6: On approval, e-card is issued

Also accessible via UMANG app.


DOCUMENTS REQUIRED

1. Aadhaar Card / PAN Card (Age & ID Proof)
2. Proof of Address
3. Mobile Number / Email
4. Caste Certificate
5. Income Certificate
6. Family Status Proof (Joint/Nuclear)
7. Ration Card (if applicable)
